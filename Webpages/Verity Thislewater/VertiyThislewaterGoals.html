Goals

Verity is a very goal oriented character, and has lots of long term goals with room for shorter-term ones that is like a Russian Nesting Doll of goals.

Long-Term Goals

Secure the Feywild's connection to the material plane.

Verity's longest term goal is to fulfill her quest to Titania, which is to shore up the Feywild’s connection to the Material Plane by restoring Fey Artifacts to the Wild Places. She worries however, that she’s getting a little too old for some of the more dangerous parts of the process, and so a secondary motivation would be to find a successor. Someone who could be Titania’s next Warlock, and next agent in the Material Plane.
