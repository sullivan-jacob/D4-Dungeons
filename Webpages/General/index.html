D4 Dungeons

This is the home page for the general section of the D4 Dungeons Dungeons and Dragons campaign website. Public campaign resources and information will be put on this page and the pages linked to it.

Contributing Content

All players are encouraged to contribute content to both the general pages and their private pages. Content contributions can be sent to Sullie over discord, and will be added as soon as it is possible to do so. Suggestions for format, styling, and structure, as well as content edits, can be sent by the same means.

Website and Github Repository Abuse Policy

Due to the public nature of this website and its associated Github repository, it is very easy to access information that is not meant for you to see. This website and your means of accessing it have been designed such that it is impossible for you to accidentally find such information. Any deliberate attempt to access such information is against the spirit of this campaign and is prohibited. Affending players will be removed from the group.
